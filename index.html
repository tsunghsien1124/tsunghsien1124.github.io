I am a 5th-year Ph.D. candidate in Economics at the Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences (GESS) at the University of Mannheim. My research interests are Macroeconomics and Computational Economics, currently focusing on Consumer Finance and Default as well as Financial Intermediation. You can find my CV and research here.

I will be joining European University Institute as a Max Weber Postdoctoral Fellow in Fall 2022, and Institute of Economics, Academia Sinica as an Assistant Research Fellow in Fall 2023.
