I am a 5th-year Ph.D. candidate in Economics at the Graduate School of Economic and Social Sciences (GESS) at the University of Mannheim. My research interests are Macroeconomics and Computational Economics, with focuses on Consumer Finance and Default as well as Financial Intermediation.

I am on the academic job market 2021/22 and will be available for interviews at the EJM and ASSA meetings.
